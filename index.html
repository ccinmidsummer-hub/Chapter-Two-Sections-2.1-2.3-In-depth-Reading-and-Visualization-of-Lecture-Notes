  • piston: BDC → TDC

Step 3

  • piston: TDC → BDC
  • ignition: spark plug ignites mixture

  • valve: exhaust valve open
  • function: spent gases are expelled

02
2.1.1 Engine Classification
There are several possible ways to classify internal combustion engines.
The classification of IC engines is given below.
内燃机有多种分类方式。下面列出的是课文中给出的几种典型分类方法。
03
Based on the Fuel Used
There are two types of engine: petrol engine, which is also called a spark-ignition engine, and diesel engine, which is also called a compression-ignition engine.
按照所用燃料来分，发动机主要有两类：汽油机，也叫火花点火式发动机；柴油机，也叫压缩点火式发动机。
04
Based on the Number of Strokes Per Cycle
Engines can be divided into two groups based on the number of piston strokes per cycle, either four or two.
An engine which needs four strokes to complete one cycle is a four-stroke cycle engine.
If it only needs two strokes to complete a cycle, it is called a two-stroke cycle engine.
Thus, a two-cycle engine fires twice as often as a four-cycle engine.
按照每个工作循环中活塞冲程的数量，发动机可以分为四冲程和二冲程两类。完成一个循环需要四个冲程的叫四冲程发动机，只需要两个冲程的叫二冲程发动机。因此，二冲程发动机的做功频率会比四冲程发动机更高。
05
Based on the Number of Cylinders
According to the number of cylinders, the engine can be divided into single-cylinder and multi-cylinder.
A single-cylinder engine is a basic piston engine configuration of an internal combustion engine.
It is often seen on motorcycles.
A multi-cylinder engine is a reciprocating internal combustion engine with multiple cylinders.
The inline-four engine is a type of inline internal combustion four-cylinder engine with all four cylinders mounted in a straight line.
按照气缸数量来分，发动机可以分为单缸发动机和多缸发动机。单缸发动机是最基本的活塞式内燃机形式，常见于摩托车。多缸发动机则具有多个气缸，其中直列四缸发动机就是把四个气缸按一条直线排列起来的一种形式。
06
Based on the Arrangement of Cylinder
The four basic cylinder arrangements of engine are inline engine, V engine, W engine, and horizontal engine.
Inline engine is the simplest and most common arrangement, with all cylinders arranged vertically in line.
If an engine has more than eight cylinders, it becomes difficult to make a sufficiently rigid frame and crankshaft with an inline arrangement.
Also, the engine becomes quite long and takes up considerable space.
The V-arrangement, with two connecting rods attached to each crankpin, permits reducing the engine length by almost one-half, thus making it much more rigid, with a stiff crankshaft.
Cylinders lying in one line are called a bank, and the angle between the banks may vary, in manufacturing practice, from 30° to 120°, the most common angles being between 40° and 75°.
A W engine is a type of engine arranged with its cylinders in a configuration in which the cylinder banks resemble the letter W, in the same way those of a V engine resemble the letter V.
A horizontal engine is a V engine with the angle between the banks increased to 180°.
This arrangement is used where there is little headroom, as in trucks.
按照气缸排列形式来分，常见的有直列式、V 型、W 型和水平对置式。直列式结构最简单、也最常见，但当气缸数超过八个时，发动机会变得很长，占用很大空间，而且机架和曲轴也更难做到足够刚性。V 型布置可以把发动机长度缩短近一半，使整体更加紧凑坚固。W 型发动机的气缸列形状像字母 W，而水平发动机可以看作两列气缸夹角扩大到 180° 的 V 型发动机，这种结构常用于顶部空间较小的车辆，如卡车。
07
Based on the Pressure Boost
According to the pressure boost, the engine can be divided into naturally aspirated engine and supercharged engine.
A naturally aspirated engine is an internal combustion engine in which air intake depends solely on atmospheric pressure.
A supercharged engine is an internal combustion engine which relies on forced induction through a turbocharger or a supercharger.
按照增压方式来分，发动机可以分为自然吸气发动机和增压发动机。自然吸气发动机的进气完全依靠大气压力，而增压发动机则通过涡轮增压器或机械增压器进行强制进气。
08
2.1.2 Four-Stroke-Cycle Petrol Engine Operation
A four-stroke petrol engine is an internal combustion engine in which the piston completes four separate strokes while turning a crankshaft.
The four separate strokes are termed intake, compression, combustion, and exhaust.
四冲程汽油机是一种内燃机，在曲轴转动的同时，活塞要完成四个独立冲程。这四个冲程分别是进气、压缩、做功（燃烧）和排气。
09
Intake
Intake is also known as induction or suction.
This stroke of the piston begins at top dead center (TDC) and ends at bottom dead center (BDC).
In this stroke, the intake valve must be in the open position while the piston pulls an air-fuel mixture into the cylinder by producing vacuum pressure through its downward motion.
进气冲程也叫吸入或吸气冲程。这个冲程中，活塞从上止点运动到下止点。进气门必须打开，活塞下行时在气缸内形成真空，把空气与燃油的混合气吸入气缸。
10
Compression
This stroke begins at BDC, or just at the end of the suction stroke, and ends at TDC.
In this stroke, the piston compresses the air-fuel mixture in preparation for ignition during the power stroke.
Both the intake and exhaust valves are closed during this stage.
压缩冲程从下止点开始，到上止点结束。在这个阶段，活塞把混合气压缩起来，为随后的点火和做功做准备。同时，进气门和排气门都处于关闭状态。
11
Combustion / Power
Combustion is also known as power or ignition.
When the piston is at TDC, the compressed air-fuel mixture is ignited by a spark plug in a petrol engine, forcefully returning the piston to BDC.
This stroke produces mechanical work from the engine to turn the crankshaft.
燃烧冲程也叫做功冲程或点火冲程。当活塞到达上止点时，汽油机中的火花塞会点燃被压缩的混合气，使活塞被强力推回下止点。这个冲程会产生机械功，并驱动曲轴旋转。
12
Exhaust
Exhaust is also known as outlet.
During the exhaust stroke, the piston once again returns from BDC to TDC while the exhaust valve is open.
This action expels the spent air-fuel mixture through the exhaust valve.
排气冲程也叫排出冲程。此时活塞再次从下止点回到上止点，排气门打开，燃烧后的废气经排气门排出气缸。
13
2.1.3 Four-Stroke-Cycle Diesel Engine Operation
A four-stroke-cycle diesel engine differs from the petrol engine by using highly compressed hot air to ignite the fuel rather than using a spark plug.
In the diesel engine, only air is initially introduced into the combustion chamber.
The high compression causes the temperature of the air to rise.
At about the top of the compression stroke, fuel is injected directly into the compressed air in the combustion chamber.
The fuel injector ensures that the fuel is broken down into small droplets, and that the fuel is distributed evenly.
The compressed air-fuel mixture is ignited by heat generated by high compression.
四冲程柴油机与汽油机的主要区别在于：它不是用火花塞点火，而是依靠高压缩产生的高温空气来点燃燃油。柴油机开始时先把空气吸入燃烧室，高压缩会使空气温度升高。到压缩冲程接近结束时，燃油被直接喷入高温高压空气中，喷油器会把燃油雾化成细小液滴并尽量均匀分布，随后混合气在高压缩产生的热量作用下自行点燃。
2.2 Crank-Connecting Rod Mechanism 重点理解三大组成部分，以及它如何把活塞的往复运动转变成曲轴的旋转运动。
默认先看图式精读；需要逐段精讲时，再展开下面的原文双语阅读卡。英文在上，中文译文在下；中文字号更小，便于学生把注意力放在英文上。
01
Warm-up
Discuss with your partner about the following passage and answer questions.
What does the crank-connecting rod mechanism consist of?
What's the function of the crank-connecting rod mechanism?
阅读下面的内容并和同伴讨论：曲柄连杆机构由什么组成？它的功能是什么？
02
Introduction
Crank-connecting rod mechanism is the engine mechanism that achieves thermal energy and mechanical energy conversion, and is used to transmit force and change movement.
Its main function is to turn the gas force on the piston into crankshaft rotation torque, so that the crankshaft rotates and moves outward.
The crank-connecting rod mechanism consists of three parts: engine block group, piston and connecting rod group, and crankshaft and flywheel group.
曲柄连杆机构是发动机中实现热能与机械能转换的重要机构，它负责传递力并改变运动形式。它的主要作用是把气体作用在活塞上的力转化为曲轴旋转所需的转矩，从而使曲轴持续旋转。课文把它分成三大部分：机体组、活塞连杆组以及曲轴飞轮组。
03
2.2.1 Engine Block Group
Engine block group includes cylinder block, crankcase, cylinder head, cylinder liner, cylinder gasket, and oil pan.
机体组包括气缸体、曲轴箱、气缸盖、气缸套、气缸垫片和油底壳。
04
Cylinder Block
The cylinder block is the supporting structure to which all other engine parts are attached.
It is usually made of iron alloy or aluminum alloy.
The cylinders guide the pistons and the lifter bores guide the lifters.
The block houses the crankshaft.
The block is heavily constructed, and such strength is necessary in maintaining correct alignment of all engine parts.
Cooling and lubricating passages are provided in the block; the lower part of the block is the upper part of the crankcase.
气缸体是发动机的基础支撑结构，其他许多零部件都安装在它上面。它通常由铁合金或铝合金制成。气缸体中的气缸用于引导活塞运动，挺柱孔用于引导挺柱，曲轴也安装在机体内部。由于它要保证各零件保持正确的装配位置，所以必须具有足够的强度。此外，机体内部还布置有冷却和润滑通道，它的下部同时构成曲轴箱的上部。
05
Cylinder Liner
A cylinder liner is a cylindrical part to be fitted into an engine block to form a cylinder.
This is called cylinder liner in Japan, but some countries call this cylinder sleeve.
It is one of the most important functional parts that make up the interior of an engine.
The cylinder liner, serving as the inner wall of a cylinder, forms a sliding surface for the piston rings while retaining the lubricant within.
The cylinder liner receives combustion heat through the piston and piston rings and transmits the heat to the coolant.
气缸套是装入发动机缸体内部并形成气缸的一种圆筒形零件。有些国家也把它叫作 cylinder sleeve。它是发动机内部非常重要的功能件之一。气缸套作为气缸内壁，不仅为活塞环提供滑动表面，还要把润滑油保持在适当范围内；同时，它还通过活塞和活塞环吸收燃烧热，并把热量传给冷却液。
06
Cylinder Gasket
A head gasket is a gasket that sits between the engine block and cylinder head in an internal combustion engine.
Its purpose is to seal the cylinders to ensure maximum compression and avoid leakage of coolant or engine oil into the cylinders.
As such, it is the most critical sealing application in any engine, and as part of the combustion chamber, it shares the same strength requirements as other combustion chamber components.
气缸垫安装在发动机缸体和气缸盖之间。它的作用是密封气缸，保证足够的压缩压力，同时防止冷却液或机油泄漏到气缸内部。因此，它是发动机中极其关键的密封部件，而且作为燃烧室的一部分，它在强度要求上也必须与其他燃烧室部件保持一致。
07
Cylinder Head
The cylinder head is a complex box part.
Intake and exhaust port holes, valve guide holes, spark plug holes for gasoline engines, or injector holes for diesel engines are processed on it.
The cylinder head is fastened to the top of the block, just as a roof fits over a house.
The underside forms the combustion chamber with the top of the piston.
气缸盖是一个结构复杂的箱体件。其上加工有进排气道孔、气门导管孔，以及汽油机的火花塞安装孔或柴油机的喷油器安装孔。气缸盖固定在缸体上方，就像房屋的屋顶一样；它的下表面与活塞顶部共同形成燃烧室。
08
2.2.2 Piston and Connecting Rod Group
Piston and connecting rod group is composed of piston, piston ring, piston pin, connecting rod and related parts.
活塞连杆组主要由活塞、活塞环、活塞销、连杆以及相关零件组成。
09
Pistons
Most gasoline engines are equipped with aluminum alloy pistons.
The piston is a cylindrical shaped hollow part that moves up and down inside the engine cylinder.
The piston is composed of piston head, piston land, piston skirt and piston pin bore.
大多数汽油机都采用铝合金活塞。活塞是一个在气缸内上下运动的中空圆柱形零件，主要由活塞顶、活塞环岸、活塞裙部和活塞销孔等部分构成。
10
Piston Head, Land, and Skirt
Piston head may have various top designs such as flat, concave, and convex, or any one of a great variety of shapes to match the combustion chamber design in the cylinder head.
Piston ring grooves are cut into the sides of the piston just below the piston head.
The lower grooves often have holes or slots in the bottom of the groove to permit oil drainage from behind the rings.
The piston lands are parts of piston between the ring grooves.
The lands provide a sealing surface for the sides of piston rings.
Piston skirt forms a bearing area in contact with the cylinder wall.
It is the area from the lower ring to the bottom of the piston.
活塞顶部可以设计成平顶、凹顶、凸顶等多种形状，以适配气缸盖中的燃烧室结构。活塞头部下方的侧面开有活塞环槽，较低位置的环槽底部通常设有孔或槽，以便把活塞环后方多余的机油排走。活塞环岸是各环槽之间的部分，为活塞环侧面提供密封表面。活塞裙部则与气缸壁接触，形成承压区域，其范围从最下方活塞环以下一直延伸到活塞底部。
11
Piston Pins
Piston pins are manufactured from hard steel.
One of the most common methods of mounting the pin in the piston and connecting rod is to press the pin into the rod and allow it to rotate in the piston with a precise clearance.
活塞销一般由硬钢制成。常见的装配方式之一是把活塞销压入连杆，并让它在活塞中以精确的配合间隙进行转动。
12
Piston Rings
The piston rings fit into the piston ring grooves and provide seals between the piston and the cylinder walls.
Piston rings may be classified as compression rings and oil rings.
Many pistons have two compression rings in the upper ring grooves and one oil ring in the lower groove.
The compression rings prevent compression leakage.
The oil rings scrape the oil from the cylinder walls, and the excess oil flows into the oil pan.
活塞环安装在活塞环槽中，在活塞与气缸壁之间起密封作用。活塞环可分为压缩环和油环。许多活塞在上部环槽中装有两道压缩环，在下部环槽中装有一道油环。压缩环的作用是防止气缸漏气，而油环负责刮除气缸壁上多余的机油，并让多余机油回流到油底壳。
13
Connecting Rods
Many connecting rods are steel-forged.
The connecting rod is attached to the crankshaft at one end and to the piston at the other end.
It is composed of connecting rod small end, connecting rod shank, connecting rod big end, connecting rod cap, and connecting rod bearing half shells.
许多连杆采用钢锻件制造。连杆一端连接曲轴，另一端连接活塞。它通常由小头、杆身、大头、连杆盖以及连杆轴承半瓦等部分组成。
14
2.2.3 Crankshaft and Flywheel Group
Crankshaft and flywheel group comprises crankshaft, crankshaft bearing, and flywheel.
曲轴飞轮组主要由曲轴、曲轴轴承和飞轮组成。
15
Crankshaft
The crankshaft serves to change the reciprocating motion of the piston into rotary motion and handles the entire power output.
The crankshaft therefore must be adequately strong and wear-resistant.
The crankshaft is actually made up of various parts such as crankshaft front end, main bearing journals, crank arm, bearing, counterbalance weight and flywheel end.
Crankshafts have finely drilled oil passages through which oil can flow from the main bearings to the connecting rod bearings.
The crankshaft front end carries three devices: the gear or sprocket that drives the camshaft, the vibration damper, and the belt pulley.
The pulley drives the engine water pump and generator.
曲轴的作用是把活塞的往复运动转变为旋转运动，并承担发动机全部动力输出。因此，曲轴必须足够坚固并且耐磨。曲轴由前端、主轴颈、曲柄臂、曲柄销、平衡块和飞轮端等部分组成。其内部还钻有精细的润滑油道，使机油能够从主轴承流向连杆轴承。曲轴前端安装有驱动凸轮轴的齿轮或链轮、减振器以及皮带轮，而皮带轮又会带动发动机水泵和发电机。
16
Flywheel
The flywheel is a comparatively heavy wheel bolted to the rear end of the crankshaft.
Its function is to help the engine run smoothly by absorbing some of the energy during the power stroke and releasing it during the other strokes.
Thus, the inertia of the flywheel tends to keep it turning at constant speed.
On the front face of the flywheel, there are marks and indentations used for setting the valve and ignition systems relative to prescribed positions of the crankshaft.
飞轮是用螺栓固定在曲轴后端的一只较重的轮盘。它的作用是在做功冲程中吸收一部分能量，并在其他冲程中释放出来，从而帮助发动机运转更加平稳。也就是说，飞轮的惯性有助于保持曲轴转速稳定。飞轮前表面还刻有用于配气系统和点火系统校正的标记和凹槽，以便与曲轴规定位置相对应。
2.3 The Valve Train 重点看配气机构的组成、传动链条、间隙控制与正时配合。
默认先看图式精读；需要逐段精讲时，再展开下面的原文双语阅读卡。英文在上，中文译文在下；中文字号更小，便于学生把注意力放在英文上。
01
Warm-up
Discuss with your partner about the following passage and picture, and answer questions.
What does the valve train consist of?
What's the function of the valve train?
阅读课文并结合图片讨论：配气机构由什么组成？它的功能是什么？
02
Introduction
The valve train is a mechanical system that controls operation of the valves in an internal combustion engine.
To coordinate the four-stroke cycle, the valve train opens and closes the valves respectively.
These valve movements must take place at exactly the right moments.
The opening of each valve is controlled by a camshaft.
The valve train consists of exhaust valve, intake valve, rocker arms, push rods, lifters, camshaft, camshaft actuator, timing chain and so on.
配气机构是内燃机中控制气门工作的机械系统。为了配合四冲程循环，它必须按照准确的时刻开启和关闭进排气门。每个气门的开启都受凸轮轴控制。课文列出的主要部件包括排气门、进气门、摇臂、推杆、挺柱、凸轮轴、凸轮轴执行机构以及正时链条等。
03
Valve Assembly
A valve assembly is a complete valve mechanism including valve, spring, spring retainer, valve locks and valve guide seal.
一个完整的气门总成通常包括气门、气门弹簧、弹簧座、锁夹以及气门导管油封等部件。
04
Exhaust Valve and Intake Valve
Valves are usually made from a steel alloy.
Exhaust valves must withstand extremely high temperatures.
The valve face must seat evenly against the valve seat in the cylinder head to prevent leaks from the cylinder during the compression and power strokes.
气门通常由合金钢制成，其中排气门必须能够承受极高的温度。气门面必须与气缸盖上的气门座均匀贴合，这样在压缩冲程和做功冲程中才能防止气缸漏气。
05
Valve Spring
Poppet valves typically require small coil springs, appropriately named valve springs, to keep them closed when not actuated by the camshaft.
They are attached to the valve stem ends, seating within spring retainers.
提升式气门通常需要小型螺旋弹簧，也就是气门弹簧，在没有受到凸轮轴驱动时保持关闭状态。弹簧安装在气门杆末端，并安置在弹簧座中。
06
Valve Drive Group
The camshaft pushes on the lifter, which pushes on the pushrod, which pushes on the rocker arm, which rotates and pushes down on the valve.
The camshaft is located above the valves within the cylinder head, and operates directly on the valve lifter.
气门传动组的工作链条是：凸轮轴推动挺柱，挺柱推动推杆，推杆推动摇臂，摇臂旋转后再向下压动气门。课文中强调，凸轮轴位于气缸盖中的气门上方，并且直接作用于气门挺柱。
07
Camshaft
While today some engines rely on a single camshaft per cylinder, which is known as a single overhead camshaft (SOHC), most modern engines are driven by two camshafts per cylinder head, one camshaft for the intake valves and another for the exhaust valves, which is known as a double or dual overhead cam (DOHC).
有些发动机采用单顶置凸轮轴，即 SOHC；而大多数现代发动机采用双顶置凸轮轴，即 DOHC，其中一根凸轮轴控制进气门，另一根控制排气门。
08
Cam Lobes
The key parts of any camshaft are the lobes.
As the camshaft spins, the lobes open and close the intake and exhaust valves in time with the motion of the piston.
There is a direct relationship between the shape of the cam lobes and the way the engine performs in different speed ranges.
任何凸轮轴的关键部分都是凸轮凸角。随着凸轮轴旋转，这些凸角会配合活塞运动的节奏，控制进排气门开启和关闭。凸轮形状与发动机在不同转速范围内的性能表现有直接关系。
09
Pushrod
Pushrods are long, slender metal rods seated within the engine block.
A push rod is a part of an internal combustion engine that rests on the top of a valve lifter and goes up into the rocker arm.
As the lifter follows the cam lobe, the push rod actuates the rocker arm and moves the valve, opening and closing it to allow fuel and air in and exhaust out of the combustion chamber.
推杆是安装在发动机缸体内的细长金属杆件。它下端支承在挺柱上，上端伸入摇臂。当挺柱跟随凸轮运动时，推杆便会推动摇臂，从而带动气门启闭，使可燃混合气或空气进入燃烧室，并让废气排出。
10
Valve Lifter
At the bottom ends, the pushrods are fitted with lifters, either solid or hydraulic, upon which the camshaft, located within the cylinder block, makes contact.
A hydraulic valve lifter is a device for maintaining zero valve clearance in an internal combustion engine.
Solid valve lifters require regular adjusting to maintain a small clearance between the valve and its rocker or cam follower.
This space prevents the parts from binding as they expand with the engine's heat, but can also lead to noisy operation and increased wear as the parts rattle against one another until they reach operating temperature.
推杆下端装有挺柱，挺柱可以是机械式的，也可以是液压式的，位于缸体内部的凸轮轴会与它接触。液压挺柱能够自动保持零气门间隙；而机械挺柱则需要定期调整，以保证气门与摇臂或从动件之间留有适当的小间隙。这个间隙可以防止零件受热膨胀后卡死，但也可能在发动机尚未达到正常工作温度前产生噪声和磨损。
11
Valve Clearance
When the engine runs in the compression stroke and power stroke, the valves must close tightly on their seats to produce a gas-tight seal and thus prevent gases escaping from the combustion chamber.
If the valves do not close fully, the engine will not develop full power.
Also, the valve heads will be liable to be burnt by the passing hot gases, and there is the likelihood of the piston crown touching an open valve, which can seriously damage the engine.
So that the valves can close fully, some clearance is needed in the operating mechanism.
This means that the operating mechanism must be able to move sufficiently far enough away from the valve to allow the valves to be fully closed against their seats by the valve spring.
However, if the clearance is set too great, this will cause a light metallic tapping noise.
在压缩冲程和做功冲程中，气门必须严密贴合在气门座上，形成气密封闭，防止燃烧室中的气体泄漏。如果气门关闭不严，发动机就无法输出全部功率；同时，气门头可能会被高温气体烧蚀，活塞顶部也有可能碰到未完全关闭的气门，从而造成严重损坏。正因为如此，配气机构中必须留有适当的气门间隙，让气门在弹簧作用下能够完全关闭。但如果间隙过大，又会产生轻微的金属敲击声。
12
Timing Belt, Chain and Gear
Camshafts actuate the valves at the appropriate time in the combustion cycle.
In order to accomplish this, the camshaft is linked to and kept in synchronisation with the crankshaft through the use of a metal chain, rubber belt, or gear.
Because these mechanisms are essential to the proper timing of valve actuation, they are named timing chains, timing belts, and timing gears, respectively.
凸轮轴必须在燃烧循环的合适时刻驱动气门动作。为此，凸轮轴需要通过金属链条、橡胶皮带或齿轮与曲轴保持同步。正因为这些部件对气门动作正时至关重要，它们分别被称为正时链条、正时皮带和正时齿轮。
13
Valve Overlap
For a period, both of the valves are open.
This period of time is known as valve overlap.
The closing of the exhaust valve laps over the opening of the intake valve.
During this time, the new mixture pushes the burned gases out the exhaust valve.
Valve overlap is held to a minimum on turbo-charged engines.
This prevents the intake charge from being blown out through the exhaust.
在某一小段时间内，进气门和排气门会同时开启，这段时间就叫作气门重叠。也就是说，排气门还未完全关闭时，进气门已经开始开启。此时，新鲜混合气会帮助把燃烧后的废气从排气门推出去。在涡轮增压发动机中，气门重叠通常会尽量减小，以防止新进入的气体直接从排气侧流失。
